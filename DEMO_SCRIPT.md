# 🎬 Demo Video Script - Skill-E

## 📋 Video Info
- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds
- **Platform:** YouTube
- **Audience:** Hackathon judges
- **Goal:** Show what Skill-E does and how it works

---

## 📝 Script

### [0-10s] Hook
**[Show Skill-E logo]**

**Voice:** "Skill-E. Turn your screen demonstrations into AI agent skills."

---

### [10-40s] The Demo
**[Screen recording of the app]**

1. **Click "Record"** - Show toolbar, click record button
2. **Do a simple task** - Example: Open Notepad → Type "Hello" → Save file
3. **Speak naturally** - "I'm opening Notepad, typing hello, and saving..."
4. **Click "Stop"** - Show processing screen
5. **Show result** - SKILL.md preview appears

**Voice:** "Just record your screen, do the task, and talk. Skill-E captures everything - clicks, screens, and your voice. Then it generates a structured skill file automatically."

---

### [40-60s] The Result
**[Show the generated SKILL.md file]**

**Voice:** "The result? A clean skill definition your AI agent can execute. With steps, prerequisites, and expected outcomes."

---

### [60-75s] Tech Stack
**[Quick text overlay]**
- Tauri 2.0
- React 19
- Rust
- Local Whisper

**Voice:** "Built with Tauri and React. 100% local - your data never leaves your machine."

---

### [75-90s] Outro
**[Show final screen with logo]**

**Voice:** "Skill-E. From demonstration to skill in seconds. Created for Dynamous Kiro Hackathon 2026."

**[Text on screen: github.com/veefoscax/Skill-E]**

---

## 🎨 Production Tips

### Recording Setup
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080
- **Cursor:** Highlight/enlarge for visibility
- **Audio:** Clear voice, no background noise

### Editing
- Fast cuts between steps
- Zoom in on important clicks
- Subtitles optional but recommended

### Music
- Optional: Low volume lo-fi background
- Or: No music, just voice

---

## ✅ Checklist Before Recording

- [ ] Clean desktop (minimal icons)
- [ ] Close notifications/apps
- [ ] Test microphone
- [ ] Practice the flow once
- [ ] Have Skill-E running and ready

---

**Keep it simple. Show, don't tell. Good luck! 🚀**
